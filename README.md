# Travel Buddy
## It's always better with more people

Travel Buddy is a platform that aims to inspire people to fullfil their dream of travelling. Travelers and Travel Agencies come together to build a community of safe, adventurous and companioned travel experience. It's environment is simple, functional and user-friendly, allowing users to create profiles, propose or search for available trips and participate in groups of people with similar interests in just a few steps.

Why travel alone when you can have a travel buddy? It's always better with more people!
